# Bagaha Assembly Constituency

The Bagaha assembly constituency (No. 4) is located in the Paschim Champaran district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Valmiki Nagar Lok Sabha constituency. Ram Singh of the BJP currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Ram Singh (BJP) won the seat with 90,013 votes. Jayesh Mangalam Singh (INC) was the runner-up with 59,993 votes. The victory margin was 30,020 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Raghaw Sharan Pandey (BJP) won the seat with 74,476 votes. Bhishm Sahani (JD(U)) was the runner-up with 66,293 votes. The victory margin was 8,183 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Prabhat Ranjan Singh (JD(U)) won the seat with 67,510 votes. Ram Prasad Yadav (RJD) was the runner-up with 18,455 votes. The victory margin was 49,055 votes.

The constituency has seen victories by Prabhat Ranjan Singh in 2010, Raghaw Sharan Pandey in 2015, and Ram Singh in 2020. The victory margins were 49,055 votes in 2010, 8,183 votes in 2015, and 30,020 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 67,510 in 2010, 74,476 in 2015, and 90,013 in 2020.
