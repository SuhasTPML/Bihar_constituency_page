# Thakurganj Assembly Constituency

The Thakurganj assembly constituency (No. 53) is located in the Kishanganj district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Kishanganj Lok Sabha constituency. Saud Alam of the RJD currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Saud Alam (RJD) won the seat with 79,909 votes. Gopal Kumar Aggarwal (IND) was the runner-up with 56,022 votes. The victory margin was 23,887 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Naushad Alam (JD(U)) won the seat with 74,239 votes. Gopal Kumar Agrawal (LJP) was the runner-up with 66,152 votes. The victory margin was 8,087 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Naushad Alam (LJP) won the seat with 36,372 votes. Gopal Kumar Agrawal (JD(U)) was the runner-up with 29,409 votes. The victory margin was 6,963 votes.

The constituency has seen victories by Naushad Alam in 2010, Naushad Alam in 2015, and Saud Alam in 2020. The victory margins were 6,963 votes in 2010, 8,087 votes in 2015, and 23,887 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 36,372 in 2010, 74,239 in 2015, and 79,909 in 2020.
