# Mahishi Assembly Constituency

The Mahishi assembly constituency (No. 77) is located in the Saharsa district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Madhepura Lok Sabha constituency. Gunjeshwar Sah of the JD(U) currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Gunjeshwar Sah (JD(U)) won the seat with 66,316 votes. Gautam Krishna (RJD) was the runner-up with 64,686 votes. The victory margin was 1,630 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Abdul Ghafoor (RJD) won the seat with 56,436 votes. Chandan Kumar Sah (RLSP) was the runner-up with 30,301 votes. The victory margin was 26,135 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Abdul Ghafoor (RJD) won the seat with 39,158 votes. Raj Kumar Sah (JD(U)) was the runner-up with 37,441 votes. The victory margin was 1,717 votes.

The constituency has seen victories by Abdul Ghafoor in 2010, Abdul Ghafoor in 2015, and Gunjeshwar Sah in 2020. The victory margins were 1,717 votes in 2010, 26,135 votes in 2015, and 1,630 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 39,158 in 2010, 56,436 in 2015, and 66,316 in 2020.
