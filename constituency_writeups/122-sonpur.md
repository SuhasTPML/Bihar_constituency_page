# Sonpur Assembly Constituency

The Sonpur assembly constituency (No. 122) is located in the Saran district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Saran Lok Sabha constituency. Ramanuj Prasad Yadav of the RJD currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Ramanuj Prasad Yadav (RJD) won the seat with 73,247 votes. Vinay Kumar Singh (BJP) was the runner-up with 66,561 votes. The victory margin was 6,686 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Ramanuj Prasad Yadav (RJD) won the seat with 86,082 votes. Vinay Kumar Singh (BJP) was the runner-up with 49,686 votes. The victory margin was 36,396 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Vinay Kumar Singh (BJP) won the seat with 64,676 votes. Rabri Devi (RJD) was the runner-up with 43,991 votes. The victory margin was 20,685 votes.

The constituency has seen victories by Vinay Kumar Singh in 2010, Ramanuj Prasad Yadav in 2015, and Ramanuj Prasad Yadav in 2020. The victory margins were 20,685 votes in 2010, 36,396 votes in 2015, and 6,686 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 64,676 in 2010, 86,082 in 2015, and 73,247 in 2020.
