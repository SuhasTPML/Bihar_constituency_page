# Kahalgaon Assembly Constituency

The Kahalgaon assembly constituency (No. 155) is located in the Bhagalpur district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Bhagalpur Lok Sabha constituency. Pawan Kumar Yadav of the BJP currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Pawan Kumar Yadav (BJP) won the seat with 115,538 votes. Shubhanand Mukesh (INC) was the runner-up with 72,645 votes. The victory margin was 42,893 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Sadanand Singh (INC) won the seat with 64,981 votes. Niraj Kumar Mandal (LJP) was the runner-up with 43,752 votes. The victory margin was 21,229 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Sadanand Singh (INC) won the seat with 44,936 votes. Kahkashan Perween (JD(U)) was the runner-up with 36,001 votes. The victory margin was 8,935 votes.

The constituency has seen victories by Sadanand Singh in 2010, Sadanand Singh in 2015, and Pawan Kumar Yadav in 2020. The victory margins were 8,935 votes in 2010, 21,229 votes in 2015, and 42,893 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 44,936 in 2010, 64,981 in 2015, and 115,538 in 2020.
