# Nalanda Assembly Constituency

The Nalanda assembly constituency (No. 176) is located in the Nalanda district of Bihar. It is a General seat under the Nalanda Lok Sabha constituency. Shrawan Kumar of the JD(U) currently represents this constituency.

In the 2020 Bihar Assembly elections, Shrawan Kumar (JD(U)) won the seat with 66,066 votes. Kaushalendra Kumar (JVP) was the runner-up with 49,989 votes. The victory margin was 16,077 votes.

In the 2015 Bihar Assembly elections, Shrawan Kumar (JD(U)) won the seat with 72,596 votes. Kaushlendra Kumar (BJP) was the runner-up with 69,600 votes. The victory margin was 2,996 votes.

In the 2010 Bihar Assembly elections, Shrawan Kumar (JD(U)) won the seat with 58,067 votes. Arun Kumar (RJD) was the runner-up with 37,030 votes. The victory margin was 21,037 votes.

Shrawan Kumar has won this constituency in all three elections between 2010 and 2020, representing JD(U) in each election. The victory margins were 21,037 votes in 2010, 2,996 votes in 2015, and 16,077 votes in 2020. The winning candidate's vote count was 58,067 in 2010, 72,596 in 2015, and 66,066 in 2020.
